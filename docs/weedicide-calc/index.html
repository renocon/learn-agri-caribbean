⚠️

Read before you mix

  • Your product label is the authority. This calculator is a guide — always follow label instructions first.
  • Wear gloves, eye protection, and a face mask when measuring, mixing, and spraying.
  • Always add concentrate to water — never pour water into concentrate.
  • Keep children, pets, and bystanders away from the spray area during and after application.
  • Observe the re-entry interval stated on your label before re-entering treated areas.
  • Store weedicide in its original, labelled container. Never store in food or drink bottles.
  • Dispose of empty containers responsibly — do not re-use or burn them.
